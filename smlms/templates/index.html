Soil Mechanics Laboratory

Soil Mechanics is of utmost importance, moreover since it the basis of foundation and super structure designs. That’s where our well equipped Soil Mechanics Lab solves the requirements. Our Soil mechanics laboratory is ISO 9001:2015 accredited and is certified by NABL.

Based in Debre Berhan, our computerized Soil Mechanics laboratory is operated by able and well experienced engineers and technicians who arrive at legitimate conclusions via lab tests on soil, rock and water.

About Us

Geotechnical Test Equipment is manufactured to the best standard and quality components, which meets our customer needs to all international standards, (EN, ASTM, AASHTO, BS, AFNOR, DIN, UNE, UNI, ISO). Moreover we have the facility to custom design and produce any equipment to any specification and standard required.

With our strong focus and goal of the future, our company strives to achieve many breakthroughs, establishing greater platforms for continued constant growth by staying true to our values and commitment to serve our customers with the best material testing equipment that meets the latest standards, quality and technology.

Geotechnical Test Equipment is founded to be one of the worlds leading manufacturer of material testing equipment. We established operation worldwide in more than 190 countries. Represented either by a network of local partners, resellers and distributers or directly though our regional sales offices.

Geotechnical Test Equipment has been operating for more than 15 years, in designs, manufacturing and sale of civil engineering and material testing equipment, tools and components. Offering best solutions for on site and laboratory tests for concrete, cement, mortar, bitumen, asphalt, soil, aggregates, rocks and steel. Geotechnical Test Equipment is manufactured in the United Kingdom and Europe. With its commercial head office located in England, UK and other regional offices located in different cotenants around the world to better serve its customers.
